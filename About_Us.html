About Izwe Lempumelelo

IZWE LEMPUMELELO LTD (PTY), which means “WORLD OF SUCCESS”, is a proudly South African construction company founded in 2022 by Mr. Sibusiso Mtshali.

Our mission is to provide a quality working environment and empower youth (aged 18–35) by creating sustainable job opportunities.

We proudly serve the government, private, and mining sectors, having completed successful projects across all. We are fully registered with CIPC and hold compliance documents such as CIDB, CSD, and TAX PIN.

Our commitment to excellence and professionalism is at the heart of everything we do.
